About Me

I am a southern girl, raised navigating hot asphalt with barefeet on hot summer days. Most days if I'm not working, you will find me on the river kayaking and fishing.

My name can tell you a lot about me. I was born and raised in Franklin, TN where my parents taught me to say yes ma'am, no ma'am. I am a former gymnast. I competed for six years and won national and state titles. I love to take pictures. Photography is a way that I can esacpe the real world. When I photograph, I lose all track of time. Seeing through a lens and capturing special moments bring me joy.
